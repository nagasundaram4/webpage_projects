About AD-HOC FM

AD-HOC HUB Technology offers you complete solution packages from consultation to turn-key installation, so you benefit as quickly as possible from using the software. FM software for facility management and maintenance from AD-HOC HUB, supports sectors such as airports, industry, hospitality, municipalities, hospitals, logistics companies, banks and universities, government departments. And you!

Our Solutions offer the right answers. We will analyze your organization and identify the requirements your CAFM system has to meet. We check the quality of your existing data. We define a meaningful data structure together with you and develop a consistent system. We identify relevant, system based metrics with you. In doing this, we create exactly the CAFM-system to suite your needs. Of course we will be there for you after installation too. We will be by your side until the work becomes routine, and beyond that.

You get all the tools to fulfill your various legal obligations e.g. operator liability, risk analysis etc. Since each company is unique in its business processes and in defining its objectives, more than software itself is required.
